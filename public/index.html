✦ Process

Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

From signup to running your first chama meeting — it takes less than 10 minutes.

1

Create Your Chama

Sign up in seconds. Give your chama a name, set your contribution cycle, and configure the rules that matter to your group.

2

Invite Members

Send invite links via WhatsApp or SMS. Members join with one tap, complete their profile, and link their M-Pesa number instantly.

3

Track & Grow

Collect contributions automatically via M-Pesa, manage loans, run meetings with AI-assisted minutes, and watch your wealth compound.
